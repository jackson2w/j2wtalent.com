How I Work

Every engagement starts with the belief that engaged and aligned teams produce the best results.

My job is to help teams find harmony. I work at the intersection of leadership, learning, and the shifting realities of modern work.

01

Leadership Development for Times of Transition

Organizations don't stall because their people lack talent; they stall because the moment has changed and the way people lead hasn't caught up. I design and facilitate leadership experiences that help managers and senior contributors reflect on who they are under pressure. Together, we build the clarity, courage, and self-awareness to lead when the ground is shifting.

This work is reflective, interactive, and built for the tensions your leaders are sitting with right now.

Ideal for: Leadership cohorts, emerging leader programs, management development, team offsites during periods of reorganization or strategic change.

02

Leadership Presence & Storytelling

Competence is table stakes. The leaders who move rooms and organizations are clear about who they are, what they stand for, and how they show up when it counts. Most leaders have never been asked to articulate that with any precision.

I work with leaders and teams to uncover and sharpen their leadership narrative; the story of how they lead, what people rely on them for, and how they create value in moments of pressure and ambiguity. This isn't personal branding. Together, we invest in the work of knowing ourselves well enough to lead others through what's next.

Ideal for: Senior leaders preparing for expanded roles, teams building trust and shared identity, high-potentials finding their voice, organizations investing in succession readiness.

03

Facilitation & Strategic Convenings

The stakes are too high for a standard meetings. Whether it's a leadership retreat that needs to actually shift something, a difficult cross-functional conversation, or a high-visibility event that demands energy and substance — I design and facilitate experiences that move people from where they are to where they need to be.

I bring warmth, precision, and enough range to hold a room of 30 or a stage of 300.

Ideal for: Executive retreats, team alignment sessions, conference keynotes and emcee engagements, panel moderation, all-hands facilitation.

About

Willie Jackson

Founder of Jackson Talent Strategies, a leadership development and facilitation consultancy. I partner with organizations navigating change, transition, and the messy human realities of building something that matters.

Over the past decade, I've designed and led leadership experiences for teams at Google, Nike, Pfizer, Meta, Verizon, Johnson & Johnson, Bloomberg, Wharton, Hilton, and others. My approach is grounded in a belief that the most powerful leadership development doesn't happen through content delivery — it happens when people are given the space, the structure, and the provocation to see themselves and their circumstances more clearly.

Based in Dallas, TX.
